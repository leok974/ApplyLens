ApplyLens Companion — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-11-12

ApplyLens Companion ("the extension") helps you speed up job applications by reading visible fields on job application pages, drafting short answers from your ApplyLens profile, and assisting with recruiter outreach.

What the extension collects

Application metadata (optional): company, role, job URL, timestamps, only when you click actions like "Scan form & suggest" or "Log application".

Outreach metadata (optional): recruiter name (you provide), LinkedIn profile URL, message preview, timestamp, only when you choose to log it.

Profile fetch: the extension requests your ApplyLens profile from your API (/api/profile/me) to tailor answers.

Diagnostics: non-PII error messages may be shown to help you debug connectivity.

What we do not collect

  • Email contents, Gmail credentials, OAuth tokens
  • Sensitive personal info (government ID, bank details)
  • Keystrokes or clipboard contents (except when you explicitly copy the drafted message)
  • Background browsing history

How we use the data

To generate suggested answers, to let you log applications/outreach to your ApplyLens account, and to show recent activity. We do not sell your data or share it with advertisers.

Data retention & deletion

Logged entries live in your ApplyLens backend and follow your retention rules. You can delete entries in ApplyLens; deletion removes them from our systems. The extension stores only minimal transient state.

Security

All communications use HTTPS. CSRF protection is enforced for browser flows; machine-to-machine calls use API keys. Access control is handled by your ApplyLens backend.

Your choices

Use autofill without logging anything. Disable host permissions or remove the extension at any time in your browser.
